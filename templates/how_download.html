How Download?

Instructions

  1. Paste the link of the YouTube video you want to download.
  2. Click "Search" button to begin search process.
  3. Select the video/audio format you want to download, then click "Download" button.
  4. Wait for the download to complete.

Where is my video?

Once your download is complete you will be able to find your video in a folder inside your download directory with the name "VideoDownloader".

For example: Downloads/VideoDownloader/My_video.mp4
